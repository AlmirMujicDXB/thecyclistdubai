Best Cycling Tracks

Dubai offers a variety of cycling tracks suitable for all skill levels. From scenic coastal routes to challenging mountain trails, there's something for every cyclist.

  • Al Qudra Cycle Track

    A stunning desert cycling experience with dedicated tracks through the beautiful Seih Al Salam desert landscape.

    Level: Flat, smooth tarmac, mostly car-free
    Distance: More than 100km, with 86km, 50km & 30km options
    Bike Type: Road, hybrid, or mountain bikes all suitable
    Water Stations: Available but widely spaced; carry own supply
    Scenery: Desert dunes, occasional wildlife (oryx, gazelles)
    Facilities: Rest areas, bike rental, lit for night cycling, coffee shops
    Seih Al Salam Desert Gallery View on Google Maps

  • Nad Al Sheba Cycle Park

    8.5 km floodlit track with varying difficulty levels and beautiful skyline views. Perfect for evening rides with friends or solo training sessions.

    Level: Beginner to intermediate, well-lit
    Distance: 9.5km circuit with breat areas
    Surface: Smooth asphalt, excellent condition
    Lighting: Fully illuminated for night cycling
    Scenery: City skyline views, Burj Khalifa view
    Facilities: Parking, toilets, shower, water stations, coffee shops bike rental
    Nad Al Sheba Gallery View on Google Maps

  • Mushrif Cycling Track

    Over 50 km track inside Mushrif National Park. Forest of more than 77000 trees and habitat for indigenous species like Arabian gazelles, foxes, and hares, along with numerous resident and migratory birds.

    Level: Hilly, moderate and easy tracks, Perfect for both experienced riders and families
    Distance: 14km green track, 17km blue track, 17km red track
    Surface: Gravel rack with ramps
    Scenery: Nature, wildlife forest, national park
    Best Time: Early morning or sunset
    Facilities: Rest areas, cafes nearby, bike rental
    Mushrif Park Gallery View on Google Maps

  • Hatta MTB Cycling Trails

    A world-class 50+ km network of scenic trails for mountain biking.

    Level: Green (Beginner), Blue (Intermediate), Red (Advanced), Black (Expert).
    Distance: 50km trails
    Surface: All level MTB trails
    Lighting: Not available
    Scenery: Stunning routes with views of Hatta Dam, rocky landscapes, and valleys
    Facilities: Parking, water fountains, coffee shop, rental
    Hatta MTB Trails Gallery View on Google Maps

Cycling Rules in Dubai

To ensure safety for all road users, Dubai has specific regulations for cyclists. Following these rules is essential for safe and legal cycling.

  • Mandatory Safety Gear

    All cyclists must wear a helmet. Reflective clothing is required for night cycling.

  • Designated Cycling Paths

    Cyclists must use dedicated cycling tracks where available. Riding on sidewalks is prohibited.

  • Traffic Rules Compliance

    Cyclists must obey all traffic signals and signs. Use hand signals when turning.

  • Bicycle Requirements

    Bicycles must have front and rear lights, reflectors, and a working bell or horn.
